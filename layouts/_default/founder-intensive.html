Frequently Asked Questions

No. The framework works in any system. Obsidian is my tool of choice, but you can adapt to Notion, Evernote, or paper.

This isn't religious. It's contemplative practice. If you meditate, journal, or have any reflective practice, you're aligned. If you're completely closed to inner work, this won't work.

What if I can't make a session?

We reschedule. But chronic no-shows indicate lack of commitment and may result in exit from cohort.

Can I get a refund?

No refunds after acceptance. This is a commitment. If you're unsure, don't apply.

What happens after 90 days?

You're independent. You have the system, the vault, and the practices. Month 6 check-in ensures sustainability. After that, you're on your own (which is the point).

What if I'm not selected?

You'll be offered Q3 waitlist or directed to The Vault/System to build foundation first.
